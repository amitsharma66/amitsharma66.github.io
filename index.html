SPEAK — Logo Design Case Study

Project brief:
SPEAK is a voice-based platform focused on communication, podcasts, and public expression. The goal was to create a logo that feels confident, modern, and easy to recognize.

Design approach:
I combined a microphone symbol with speech bubbles to visually represent voice and conversation. The typography was kept bold and clean to ensure clarity across digital platforms.

Final result:
A simple, memorable logo that communicates “Speak up, stand out” and works well across websites, social media, and branding materials.
